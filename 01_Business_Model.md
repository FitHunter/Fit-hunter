# FitWell — Business Model & Monetization

> Last Updated: April 14, 2026

---

## Overview

FitWell is a multi-sided marketplace with three distinct user groups:
- **Consumers** — people searching for gyms and fitness professionals
- **Fitness Professionals** — personal trainers, coaches, nutritionists
- **Gyms & Studios** — facilities looking to attract members and trainers

The monetization strategy is built around charging the supply side (professionals and gyms) while keeping the platform free for consumers. This mirrors the proven Angie's List / Yelp / Thumbtack model.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Trainer & Coach Subscriptions (Primary Revenue)
Fitness professionals pay a monthly or annual subscription to maintain an active, visible profile.

| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0/mo | Basic profile, name + certifications only, no reviews shown, not searchable |
| **Starter** | $19/mo | Full profile, reviews visible, searchable, 1 specialty listed |
| **Pro** | $39/mo | Everything in Starter + VSL video upload, contact form, booking link, priority search placement, up to 5 specialties |
| **Elite** | $69/mo | Everything in Pro + featured badge, top of search results in their area, analytics dashboard (profile views, contact requests) |

> **Why this works:** Trainers already pay for leads. ClassPass charges gyms, Thumbtack charges per lead. A flat subscription at $39/mo is easy to justify if it brings even one new client per month.

---

### 2. Gym & Studio Subscriptions (Primary Revenue)
Gyms pay to claim, maintain, and enhance their profile.

| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|---|---|---|
| **Unclaimed** | $0 | Auto-generated stub listing, no photos, no contact info |
| **Basic Claim** | $49/mo | Claimed profile, photos, hours, amenities, contact info |
| **Verified** | $99/mo | Everything in Basic + verified badge, class listings, "Who Works Here" trainer links, review response ability |
| **Premium** | $199/mo | Everything in Verified + featured placement in search, promoted in Trainer-to-Gym matching, analytics dashboard |

> **Note:** Auto-generating gym stub listings (unclaimed) creates data density from day one and gives gyms a reason to claim their profile.

---

### 3. Trainer-to-Gym Matching — Featured Gym Placement
Gyms on the Premium tier get featured placement in the Trainer-to-Gym discovery section. This is a key differentiator and a separate value prop for gym operators who want to attract quality independent trainers.

---

### 4. Lead Generation / Pay-Per-Contact (Future)
Once the platform has sufficient traffic, introduce a pay-per-lead model as an alternative or add-on to subscriptions:
- Trainers on the Free tier pay per contact request received (e.g., $5–$15 per lead)
- This lowers the barrier to entry and captures trainers not ready to subscribe

---

### 5. Certification Verification Fees (Future)
Charge a one-time fee ($10–$25) for FitWell to verify a trainer's certifications against ACE/NASM/ISSA databases. Verified trainers get a "Certified & Verified" badge — a trust signal that drives conversions.

---

### 6. Advertising / Sponsored Placements (Future — Year 2+)
- Sponsored search results for supplement brands, fitness equipment, or gym apparel
- "Presented by" sponsorships on category pages (e.g., Weight Loss category sponsored by a nutrition brand)
- Keep this limited and clearly labeled to protect platform trust

## Pricing Philosophy

- **Consumers always free** — removing the paywall on the demand side drives traffic, which is what the supply side is paying for
- **Free tier for professionals** — a free tier ensures supply-side density before monetization; upgrade features should feel obviously valuable
- **Annual discount** — offer 2 months free on annual plans to improve LTV and reduce churn (e.g., Pro annual = $390/yr vs $468/yr monthly)

## Competitive Pricing Benchmarks

| Platform | What They Charge |
|---|---|
| Thumbtack | $3–$75 per lead (pay per contact) |
| Angie's List (pros) | $300–$1,500/yr depending on category |
| FindYourTrainer | ~$30/mo trainer subscription |
| Mindbody (studios) | $129–$349/mo |
| Yelp Ads | $150–$1,000+/mo |

**FitWell's pricing is intentionally accessible** to drive early adoption, with room to increase as brand value grows.
