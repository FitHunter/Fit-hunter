> Last Updated: April 14, 2026

## What Is FitWell?

FitWell is a fitness discovery and review platform — the "Angie's List for the fitness world." It is a multi-sided marketplace that connects three audiences:

1. **Consumers** looking for gyms and fitness professionals in their area
2. **Fitness professionals** (trainers, coaches, nutritionists) looking to grow their client base
3. **Trainers looking for a gym home** — a B2B matching layer unique to FitWell

---

## The Problem We Solve

Finding the right fitness professional or gym is fragmented and frustrating. Here's what's broken today:

- **No trainer discovery:** Google and Yelp show gym reviews but have no trainer profiles, certifications, or specialties.
- **Certification sites lack reviews:** ACE and NASM directories list certified trainers but have no reviews, no gym context, and no easy app.
- **Trainer apps miss the gym layer:** Platforms like FindYourTrainer focus on trainers in isolation — no gym connection, no local community.
- **Booking apps aren't review platforms:** Mindbody and ClassPass are booking tools, not discovery platforms. They don't give you the full story on who works there.
- **Nutrition and wellness have no home:** Nutrition specialists and wellness coaches are almost completely absent from any unified directory.

**The result?** People rely on word-of-mouth, scroll through Instagram, or just walk into the nearest gym — none of which are reliable. FitWell solves this.

## Core Features

### 1. Trainer & Coach Profiles
Individual profiles for:
- Personal trainers
- Group fitness instructors
- Nutrition specialists
- Physical therapists
- Wellness coaches

Each profile includes:
- Certifications (ACE, NASM, ISSA, RD, etc.) with verification
- Specialties (weight loss, strength training, prenatal, senior fitness, sports performance, nutrition, and more)
- Client reviews and ratings specific to that trainer
- Trainer VSL (video sales letter / intro video)
- "Available in-person at [Gym Name]" or "Virtual sessions available" designation

---

### 2. Gym Profiles
Full gym listings including:
- Photos, hours, amenities, pricing, and classes offered
- Verified badge for gyms that claim and maintain their profile
- Star ratings + written reviews from real members
- **"Who Works Here" section** — linked trainer and coach profiles

---

### 3. Review Board
- Separate review feeds for gyms and individual trainers
- Verified review system — reviewers confirm they actually trained there
- Star rating by category:
  - **Gyms:** cleanliness, equipment, staff, value, results
  - **Trainers:** communication, expertise, motivation, punctuality
- Photo uploads from reviewers

---

### 4. Search & Discovery
- Search by city, zip code, or "near me"
- Filters: type (gym, trainer, nutritionist, coach), specialty, price range, rating, virtual vs. in-person, certification
- "Top Rated Near Me" and "Most Reviewed" rankings
- Category browsing: Weight Loss, Strength Training, Sports Performance, Nutrition, Recovery, etc.

---

### 5. Booking & Contact
- Direct message or contact request to trainers
- External booking link integration (trainers can link to their own scheduling tool)
- "Request a Free Consult" CTA on trainer profiles

---

### 6. Trainer-to-Gym Matching *(Unique Feature)*
A B2B discovery layer for fitness professionals seeking a gym to work out of.

**Use case:** A personal trainer moving to a new area can browse gyms and evaluate them as a business partner, not just as a facility.

Gyms can pitch trainers by showcasing:
- **Member flow** — traffic volume (more members = more potential clients)
- **Trainer-friendly environment** — quality equipment, private training space, supportive culture
- **Comp rates** — split percentage or hourly rental rates
- **Perks** — marketing support, locker room access, scheduling tools, etc.

This creates a two-way marketplace where gyms compete to attract quality trainers, and trainers can find the right business home.

## Competitive Landscape

| Platform | What It Does | What It Lacks |
|---|---|---|
| Google / Yelp | Gym reviews | No trainer profiles or certifications |
| ACE / NASM Directories | Certified trainer listings | No reviews, no gym context |
| FindYourTrainer | Trainer profiles | No gym layer or local community |
| Mindbody / ClassPass | Booking & scheduling | Not a discovery or review platform |
| Instagram | Trainer discovery (informal) | No verification, reviews, or structure |

**FitWell's edge:** The only platform that combines verified trainer profiles, gym listings, multi-category reviews, and a trainer-to-gym matching layer in one place.
