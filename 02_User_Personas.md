# FitWell — User Personas

> Last Updated: April 14, 2026

---

## Overview

FitWell serves three distinct user groups. Understanding who they are, what they want, and what frustrates them is essential for building the right product.

---

## Persona 1: The Fitness Seeker (Consumer)

**Name:** Jordan, 31
**Occupation:** Marketing manager
**Location:** Suburban area, recently moved to a new city

### Background
Jordan works a desk job, wants to get back in shape, and just relocated. She has no local network to ask for gym or trainer recommendations. She's scrolled through Instagram trainers but has no way to verify if they're legit or available locally.

### Goals
- Find a personal trainer who specializes in weight loss and can work around her schedule
- Find a gym near her apartment that's clean, well-equipped, and not intimidating
- Read honest reviews before spending money — she's been burned by a bad gym membership before
- Know if a trainer is actually certified before trusting them with her health

### Frustrations
- Google shows her gym locations but nothing about the trainers inside
- Yelp reviews are mostly about parking and front desk staff, not actual training quality
- She has no way to filter trainers by specialty or certification
- Booking a "free consult" on Instagram means giving out her number to a stranger

### How She Uses FitWell
- Searches "personal trainer near me" → filters by specialty (weight loss), certification, and rating
- Reads trainer profiles, watches a VSL, checks reviews
- Sees the trainer is available at a gym 2 miles away
- Clicks "Request a Free Consult" directly through the platform

### What Makes Her Stay
- Verified reviews she can trust
- Easy filtering so she finds the right fit fast
- Ability to contact trainers without handing over personal info upfront

## Persona 2: The Independent Trainer

**Name:** Marcus, 27
**Certification:** NASM-CPT, Corrective Exercise Specialist
**Location:** Mid-size city, works out of two gyms

### Background
Marcus has been training for 4 years. He has 18 clients and gets most of them through Instagram DMs and word of mouth. He knows he needs a better way to get discovered but doesn't have time to manage a full marketing funnel. He's frustrated that no platform gives him a real profile — just a name in a directory.

### Goals
- Get discovered by people in his area who are actually looking for a trainer
- Showcase his certifications so he stands out from uncertified "coaches"
- Collect and display client reviews in one credible place
- Grow his client base without paying per lead or running ads

### Frustrations
- ACE/NASM directories just list his name — no photo, no reviews, no bookings
- Instagram requires constant content creation to stay visible
- FindYourTrainer charges per lead which feels risky on a tight budget
- Potential clients can't tell the difference between him and someone with no certifications

### How He Uses FitWell
- Creates a Pro profile with certifications, specialties, and a VSL
- Links his Calendly for free consult bookings
- Asks existing clients to leave verified reviews
- Shows up in search results when people in his city look for strength training

### What Makes Him Stay
- He gets contact requests — the ROI is obvious
- His certifications are verified and prominently displayed
- His profile does the selling for him while he focuses on training

## Persona 3: The Gym Owner / Manager

**Name:** Diane, 44
**Business:** Mid-size independent gym, ~300 members
**Location:** Suburban city

### Background
Diane owns a gym she's run for 8 years. She's lost members to big box chains and boutique studios. She spends money on Facebook ads but can't track ROI well. She has 4 trainers who work out of her gym but nothing online that connects her gym profile to their presence.

### Goals
- Attract new members who are actively searching for a gym
- Showcase her trainers as a competitive advantage
- Find quality independent trainers looking for a gym home (she has open floor space)
- Respond to and manage her online reputation

### Frustrations
- Yelp reviews sit there with no way to tell her full story
- She has no way to promote her trainers alongside her gym listing
- Finding independent trainers to rent floor space is all word-of-mouth
- She pays for ads but loses people who can't find basic info (hours, pricing, classes)

### How She Uses FitWell
- Claims and builds out her Verified gym profile
- Links her 4 trainers' profiles under "Who Works Here"
- Opts into the Trainer-to-Gym matching section to attract independent trainers
- Responds to reviews and monitors her rating dashboard
- Gets inbound interest from trainers looking to work out of her facility

### What Makes Her Stay
- She can see profile views and inquiry volume in her dashboard
- Her gym shows up when trainers and members search her area
- The platform does gym-trainer matchmaking she's never had before

## Persona 4: The Relocating Trainer (Trainer-to-Gym Matching)

**Name:** Devon, 33
**Certification:** ISSA-CPT, Nutrition Coach
**Situation:** Moving from Chicago to Austin in 6 weeks

### Background
Devon has a strong client base in Chicago but is moving for personal reasons. He needs to rebuild his business in a new city fast. He has no contacts in Austin and no way to evaluate gyms as business partners before he arrives. He needs to know comp rates, traffic levels, and whether a gym is actually trainer-friendly before committing.

### Goals
- Find gyms in Austin that welcome independent trainers
- Compare comp structures (hourly rate vs. split) before reaching out
- Evaluate gyms based on member traffic and equipment quality
- Land a gym home before he arrives so he can hit the ground running

### Frustrations
- There's no platform that shows gyms from a trainer's business perspective
- He has to cold-call gyms and ask awkward questions about rates and policies
- He can't evaluate a gym's culture or member flow without visiting in person
- He risks signing a bad agreement because he lacks information

### How He Uses FitWell
- Opens the Trainer-to-Gym matching section, filters by Austin
- Sees gym profiles with member flow ratings, comp rates, and trainer testimonials
- Contacts two gyms through the platform before flying out
- Joins a gym that already has 3 linked trainer profiles — a good sign for culture

### What Makes Him Stay
- He found a gym home before moving — saved him weeks of stress
- He updates his FitWell profile to reflect his new Austin location
- He becomes an active member of the platform's trainer community

## Persona 5: The Nutrition Coach / Wellness Professional

**Name:** Priya, 38
**Credentials:** Registered Dietitian (RD), online coaching practice
**Location:** Works virtually, based in Denver

### Background
Priya is an RD who does 1-on-1 nutrition coaching. She has a website and some social media but is virtually invisible on any fitness discovery platform. Her ideal clients are people who've just hired a personal trainer and realize they also need nutrition support.

### Goals
- Get discovered by people who are already in fitness mode and need nutrition help
- Establish credibility through her RD credential (which many "nutrition coaches" don't have)
- Reach clients nationally since her work is virtual
- Position herself as complementary to personal trainers, not competing with them

### Frustrations
- She doesn't show up on any fitness platform — her clients find her only through Google or referrals
- Fake "nutrition coaches" with no credentials flood Instagram, diluting trust in her field
- No platform distinguishes between a Registered Dietitian and an uncertified coach

### How She Uses FitWell
- Creates a Pro profile marked "Virtual sessions available"
- Lists her RD credential with verification — it's prominently displayed
- Shows up when users filter by "Nutrition" from any city
- Gets discovered by personal trainers who refer their clients to her

### What Makes Her Stay
- First platform that actually includes nutrition professionals as first-class members
- Her verified RD credential sets her apart from uncertified coaches
- She gets referral traffic from trainers who link to her profile
